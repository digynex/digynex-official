# 💎 DigyNex Viral Intelligence Hub (VIH) v1.0

#CONTEXT:
The VIH is a specialized AI-powered dashboard designed to identify upcoming viral content niches and generate high-fidelity, production-ready assets (Prompts, Scripts, Sound Tags).

#LOGIC:
- **Phase 1: Discovery** (Uses Gemini to predict visual trends).
- **Phase 2: Architectural Prompting** (Generates 4-stage consistent visual sets).
- **Phase 3: Auditory Design** (Generates scripts and SFX metadata).

## 📁 STRUCTURE:
- [dashboard.html](file:///d:/Amila/With%20Antigravity/DigyNex-Official/VIRAL_HUB/interface/dashboard.html): The primary interactive user interface.
- [discovery.js](file:///d:/Amila/With%20Antigravity/DigyNex-Official/VIRAL_HUB/core/discovery.js): The core AI engine logic.
- [SCHEMA.md](file:///d:/Amila/With%20Antigravity/DigyNex-Official/VIRAL_HUB/docs/SCHEMA.md): Architectural definitions for future AI expansion.

## 🚀 HOW TO USE:
1. Open `dashboard.html` in your browser.
2. Click **"Auto-Discover 10 Niches"** to trigger the AI analysis.
3. Select a Niche to open the **Viral Architect** modal.
4. Copy-paste the **Image Prompts** into Midjourney/DALL-E and the **Audio Script** into an AI Voice generator.
